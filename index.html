About LetUsTech

Liverpool-based. Built on genuine expertise.

LetUsTech was founded in Liverpool by a team of IT professionals who saw a clear gap: small businesses and individuals were being underserved by overpriced, impersonal IT providers.

We started small — helping local businesses get their technology in order — and have grown steadily by doing good work and being straight with people. We don't oversell, we don't tie you into contracts you don't need, and we don't disappear after you've signed on the dotted line.
