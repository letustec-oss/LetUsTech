How it works

From launch to CSV in under a minute

1

Open the app

Double-click EmailCSVMaker.exe — no installer, no setup wizard. It launches instantly and your existing lists load automatically from the last session.

Portable — runs from anywhere, even a USB stick
2

Add your contacts

Click Add Contact and fill in the name, email, and any tags. Use the Import CSV button to bulk-load an existing list — columns are mapped automatically.

3

Organise with tags

Assign one or more tags to each contact. Filter the list view by tag to see only the segment you care about. Rename or add new tags at any time.

4

Remove duplicates

Click Deduplicator to scan the whole list. The app highlights repeated email addresses and lets you review before removing — no accidental deletions.

Keeps your first entry, flags the rest for review
5

Export your CSV

Hit Export CSV, choose your filename and location, and you're done. The output is a clean, RFC-compliant CSV — ready to drag straight into Mailchimp, HubSpot, Klaviyo, or any platform.
