Cookie Policy

Last updated: 30 June 2026 — This policy explains what cookies our website uses and how you can manage them.

Plain English summary: We use a small number of cookies — mostly for Google Analytics so we can understand how people use our site. You can block them at any time in your browser settings.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites work properly, remember your preferences, and give website owners information about how their site is being used.

2. What cookies do we use?

We use the following categories of cookies:

Essential cookies — these are required for the website to function and cannot be disabled.

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. We do not share cookie data with advertising networks.

3. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (Universal Analytics / GA4) to understand how visitors use our website — for example, which pages are most popular and how long people spend on the site. This data is aggregated and anonymised; we cannot identify you personally from it.

Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking across all websites by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

4. How to manage cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Here are links to guidance for the most common browsers:

5. Your consent

By continuing to use our website, you consent to the use of analytics cookies as described in this policy. You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing cookies in your browser or using the opt-out options described above.
